Method: FunctionStart+Statement

FunctionStart: "function "+Name+Vars
Name: <any string of letters>
Vars: LParen+RParen | LParen+VarList+Name+RParen
VarList: "" | Name+","+VarList
Body: "" | Statement+Body
Statement: IfStatement | AssignStatement | BlockStmt
IfStatement: IfToken+LParen+Expr+RParen
Expr: SumExpr LessThan SumExpr | SumExpr GreaterThan SumExpr | SumExpr EqEq SumExpr
SumExpr: ProdExpr | ProdExpr+SumExprList
SumExprList: "" | Plus+SumExpr+SumExprList | Minus+SumExpr+SumExprList
ProdExpr: Atom | Atom+ProdExprList
ProdExprList: Atom+Times+ProdExprList
Atom: <an integer> | LParen Expr RParen
AssignStatement: Name+Eq+Expr
BlockStmt: LBrace StmtList RBrace
StmtList: "" | Statement+StmtList
